Our Story

Who We Are

We exist to connect fans with the artists and experiences that move them — one show at a time.
Our Mission
Built for Live
Music Lovers

Eventify was born from a simple frustration: buying tickets to live events shouldn't be a battle. Confusing platforms, hidden fees, and sold-out screens before the page even loads — we've all been there.

So we built something better. A platform designed with fans first — fast, transparent, and full of the events you actually want to attend. No bots. No nonsense. Just great live music, made accessible.

From intimate jazz nights to 80,000-person stadium spectacles, we curate the best of live culture across the UK and Europe, and make booking feel effortless.

What We Stand For
Our Values
Speed First
Tickets move fast. So do we. Our platform is engineered to handle demand spikes at launch — meaning you're never stuck in a queue while bots clean out the floor.
Radical Transparency
The price you see is the price you pay. We display all fees upfront, no mystery charges at checkout. What you see on the listing is your final total.
Culture Over Commerce
We're music fans running a ticket platform — not the other way around. Every event we add goes through a curation filter. Quality over quantity, always.
How We Got Here
Our Journey
2019
The Idea
Founders Kai and Priya meet at a jazz club in Shoreditch — after both missing out on tickets to the same sold-out show the week before. They sketch the first version of Eventify on a napkin.
2020
Beta Launch
Despite a global pandemic, we launch quietly with 12 virtual and socially-distanced events. 3,000 early users sign up in the first month.
2021
Live Music Returns
As venues reopen across the UK, Eventify becomes the go-to booking platform for independent promoters. We partner with 60+ venues in the first six months back.
2023
European Expansion
We expand into Portugal, Germany, and the Netherlands — starting with festival partnerships including Afro Nation Porto. 100,000 tickets sold milestone reached.
2025
Half a Million Fans
Eventify now serves over 500,000 fans across 48 cities, with more than 1,200 events on the platform each year. We're just getting started.
