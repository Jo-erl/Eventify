Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment information for ticket purchases
  • Event preferences and browsing history
  • Device and usage information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send confirmations
  • Communicate with you about events and promotions
  • Personalize your experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Event organizers for event management
  • Payment processors for transaction completion
  • Service providers who assist with our operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver targeted advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Choices

You may:

  • Update or correct your account information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Disable cookies through your browser

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through email or prominent notices on our service.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@eventify.com.
